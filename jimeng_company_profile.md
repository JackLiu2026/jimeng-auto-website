## 公司简介

### 中文版本
常州吉盟机电控制系统有限公司成立于2007年1月，是一家专注于工业自动化领域的高新技术企业。公司长期致力于为制造业客户提供先进的自动化解决方案，包括工业视觉系统、安全控制设备以及欧洲知名品牌的自动化产品。

公司拥有专业的技术团队和丰富的行业经验，为客户提供从方案设计、产品选型、系统集成到售后服务的全方位支持。我们与施耐德(Schneider)、西门子(Siemens)、SIKC等国际知名品牌建立了长期稳定的合作关系，确保为客户提供原装正品和及时的技术支持。

### 英文版本
Changzhou Jimeng Mechanical & Electrical Control System Co., Ltd. was established in January 2007 as a high-tech enterprise specializing in industrial automation. The company is dedicated to providing advanced automation solutions for manufacturing clients, including industrial vision systems, safety control equipment, and automation products from renowned European brands.

With a professional technical team and extensive industry experience, we offer comprehensive support ranging from solution design, product selection, system integration to after-sales service. We have established long-term stable partnerships with internationally renowned brands such as Schneider, Siemens, and SIKC, ensuring genuine products and timely technical support for our clients.
